Plugin for jumping between the cursor history locations ... this aims to be similar to the <ALT-[left|right]> ability of QTCreator.


Mappings: 
<ALT-j> move forward in cursor history
<ALT-k> move backward in cursor history


Commands: 
CursorHistForward
CursorHistBack
CursorHistClear
